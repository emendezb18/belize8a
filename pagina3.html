There is a Victoria Peak trail in the Cockscomb Basin Wildlife Sanctuary that is open during the dry season in Belize from February to June. The trek takes approximately 3-4 days and is 29 km. 300 different bird species have been recorded in the Cockscomb Basin alone.

Along with seasonal migratory birds that make up an average of 18% of the bird population, the forests of Victoria Peak in Belize are home to numerous species native. A maximum of 8 people per group is allowed and licensed tour guides who live nearby are required to accompany hikers on the trek. There are two campsites where hikers frequently spend the night, one 12 km away and the other 19 km away.
